Mazut M100

ORIGIN: RUSSIA, IRAN, TURKMENISTAN, UZBEKISTAN, AZERBAIJAN, KAZAKHSTAN

In order to get current market price please contact our sales department following our procedures.

 

#MAZUT 100   #MAZUT GOST 10585-99  #MAZUT GOST 10585-75 

Our MAZUT 100 comes from reliable sources. Fuel oil mazut 100 (M100) is supplied according to the Russian technical specification GOST 10585-99. All grades is guaranteed to meet the specifications and to pass the requirements of SGS.

 

The grades:

”Very Low Sulphur” sulphur content of 0.5%

”Low Sulphur”  sulphur content of 0.5-1.0%

”Normal Sulphur” sulphur content of 1.0-2.0%

”High Sulphur”  sulphur content of 2.0-3.5%
